About Bensons

We have supplied hundreds of companies ranging from hospitality corporate industrial and healthcare providing uniforms that allow businesses and companies from all market segments to highlight their staff, their service, and ultimately their brand. Our vision is clear. Corporatewear and Workwear should not be boring. On the contrary, we make corporate wear and workwear that is fashionable, fun, and elegant without sacrificing its functionality.

Year after year, the business grew, and we moved from a humble small office in Limerick to the idyllic town of Midleton, in Co. Cork, into larger facilities with manufacturing capabilities. During the pandemic, Benson's stepped up to help the community of Ireland, stopping production of regular stock in order to focus on producing medical gowns. The company adapted quickly to help the national healthcare system.

The passion for design and dress making of Benson's CEO and Fashion Designer Beni Moussa began when he was a child, helping his mother and sisters to tweak dresses and make patterns for silk printing. A growing interest in the Fashion Industry led him to London, where he sourced textiles and fashion for different corporate wear companies. As part of his long term vision he moved to Ireland to open a manufacturing company specializing in uniforms where he could inspire creativity and infuse fashion into the industry.

Benson's is now a secure HSE provider, providing high-quality gowns and masks for the Irish healthcare system. Our goal is to be an integrated multi-service company offering solutions for our clients. We focus on sustainability and source our materials from ethical sources. Listening to our clients' needs is our foremost expertise.
